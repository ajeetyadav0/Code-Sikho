Timeline Animation Techniques

These examples demonstrate different timeline layouts and animation techniques:

  • Vertical Timeline: Classic timeline with dates on the left and content on the right.
  • Horizontal Timeline: Timeline that flows horizontally with dates above the content.
  • Centered Timeline: Timeline with dates and content arranged in a centered layout.

The animations use CSS keyframes to create smooth entrance effects:

  • fadeInUp - Elements fade in and move up from below.
  • fadeInDown - Elements fade in and move down from above.
  • zoomIn - Elements fade in and scale up from a smaller size.

The animation-delay property is used to stagger the animations, creating a sequential appearance effect.

Hover effects are added to the content boxes to provide interactive feedback.
