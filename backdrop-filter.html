Common backdrop-filter Values

  • blur() - Applies a Gaussian blur
  • brightness() - Adjusts brightness
  • contrast() - Adjusts contrast
  • drop-shadow() - Applies a drop shadow
  • grayscale() - Converts to grayscale
  • hue-rotate() - Changes hue
  • invert() - Inverts colors
  • opacity() - Applies transparency
  • saturate() - Adjusts color saturation
  • sepia() - Applies sepia tone

You can combine multiple filters by space-separating them:

backdrop-filter: blur(5px) contrast(150%) saturate(120%);

Note: backdrop-filter is applied to the area behind an element, not the element itself.
