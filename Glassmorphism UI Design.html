Glassmorphism UI Design

Glassmorphism is a design trend that emphasizes light or dark objects placed on top of colorful backgrounds. The objects have a frosted glass effect with transparency and blur.

Transparency

Semi-transparent backgrounds with rgba colors create the glass effect.

Backdrop Blur

The backdrop-filter property with blur creates the frosted glass appearance.

Border & Shadow

Subtle borders and shadows enhance the depth and separation of elements.
